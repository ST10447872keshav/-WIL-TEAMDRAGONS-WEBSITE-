Garden Maintenance Course

Our Garden Maintenance course teaches you essential gardening skills such as plant care, soil preparation, pest control, and sustainable garden practices. You’ll also learn to use gardening tools effectively and maintain the health and beauty of various plants and landscapes.

By the end of this course, you will be equipped with the knowledge and techniques to keep any garden flourishing, whether for personal enjoyment or as part of a professional landscaping service.
